# Auto Insurance Policy - Policy Number: AUTO-001-ALICE

**Policyholder:** Alice Johnson  
**Policy Period:** January 1, 2026 - January 1, 2027  
**Vehicle:** 2024 Honda Accord  
**Policy Type:** Standard Auto Coverage

---

## Coverage Summary

This policy provides coverage for your vehicle and liability protection. Please review all sections carefully to understand what is covered and what is excluded.

---

## Part A - Liability Coverage

### Coverage A1: Bodily Injury Liability
- **Limit:** $100,000 per person / $300,000 per accident
- **Coverage:** We will pay damages for bodily injury for which you become legally responsible because of an auto accident. This includes medical expenses, lost wages, and pain and suffering.

### Coverage A2: Property Damage Liability
- **Limit:** $50,000 per accident
- **Coverage:** We will pay for property damage for which you become legally responsible because of an auto accident. This includes damage to other vehicles, buildings, or property.

**Exclusions:**
- Damage to property owned or being transported by you
- Damage to property rented to, used by, or in your care
- Liability assumed under any contract or agreement
- Damage caused intentionally by an insured person

---

## Part B - Medical Payments Coverage

### Coverage B: Medical Payments
- **Limit:** $5,000 per person
- **Coverage:** We will pay reasonable medical and funeral expenses incurred within 3 years from the date of accident for:
  - You and family members while occupying a covered auto
  - Other persons while occupying your covered auto

**Exclusions:**
- Injury sustained while occupying a vehicle with fewer than four wheels
- Injury due to war or nuclear hazard
- Injury while committing a felony

---

## Part C - Uninsured Motorist Coverage

### Coverage C: Uninsured Motorist Bodily Injury
- **Limit:** $100,000 per person / $300,000 per accident
- **Coverage:** We will pay damages for bodily injury caused by an uninsured or hit-and-run driver.

**Exclusions:**
- Punitive or exemplary damages
- Injury to an insured person while occupying a vehicle they own that is not listed on this policy

---

## Part D - Physical Damage Coverage

### Coverage D1: Collision Coverage
- **Deductible:** $500 per accident
- **Coverage:** We will pay for direct and accidental damage to your covered auto caused by:
  - Collision with another vehicle or object
  - Upset of your covered auto (rollover)

### Coverage D2: Comprehensive (Other Than Collision) Coverage
- **Deductible:** $250 per loss
- **Coverage:** We will pay for direct and accidental loss to your covered auto from any cause except collision, including:
  - **Fire or explosion**
  - **Theft or larceny**
  - **Vandalism or malicious mischief**
  - **Hail, wind, or water damage** (excluding flood - see limitations)
  - **Falling objects**
  - **Glass breakage** (windshield, windows)
  - **Contact with bird or animal**

**Flood Damage Limitation:**
While this policy covers water damage in certain situations (such as from severe weather causing water intrusion), it does NOT cover flood damage as defined by rising water from external sources. Flood damage requires separate flood insurance coverage.

### General Physical Damage Exclusions:
- **Wear and tear**, freezing, mechanical or electrical breakdown
- **Road damage to tires** (unless caused by a covered loss)
- **Racing or speed contests** - any damage while your vehicle is being used in organized or informal racing
- **Intentional damage** - damage caused deliberately by you or someone acting on your behalf
- **Loss to electronic equipment** not permanently installed (e.g., portable GPS, radar detectors)
- **Loss due to conversion, embezzlement, or secretion** by a person in possession under a bailment lease, conditional sale, or other encumbrance
- **Commercial use** - damage occurring while vehicle is used for ride-sharing, delivery, or other commercial purposes (unless specifically endorsed)

---

## Part E - Duties After an Accident or Loss

When an accident or loss occurs, you must:
1. **Notify us promptly** of how, when, and where the accident or loss happened
2. **Cooperate with us** in the investigation, settlement, or defense of any claim
3. **Promptly send us copies** of legal papers if you're sued
4. **Submit proof of loss** within 91 days
5. **Protect your covered auto** from further damage

---

## Part F - General Provisions

### Policy Period and Territory
This policy applies only to accidents and losses that occur:
- During the policy period shown on the Declarations Page
- Within the United States, its territories and possessions, Puerto Rico, and Canada

### Who is an Insured
- You and any family member
- Any person using your covered auto with your permission
- Any person or organization legally responsible for the acts of an insured person

### Termination
We may cancel this policy only as permitted by law. You may cancel at any time by notifying us in writing.

## Common Coverage Scenarios

### ✅ Covered Scenarios:
- **Collision with another vehicle** - Collision Coverage applies (after $500 deductible)
- **Vehicle stolen from parking lot** - Comprehensive Coverage applies (after $250 deductible)
- **Windshield cracked by road debris** - Comprehensive Coverage applies (after $250 deductible)
- **Damage from hailstorm** - Comprehensive Coverage applies (after $250 deductible)
- **Hit by uninsured driver** - Uninsured Motorist Coverage applies
- **Deer collision** - Comprehensive Coverage applies (after $250 deductible)

### ❌ Not Covered Scenarios:
- **Flood damage to vehicle** - Not covered; requires separate flood insurance
- **Damage while using vehicle for Uber/Lyft** - Commercial use exclusion applies
- **Normal wear and tear, engine failure** - Maintenance exclusions apply
- **Damage during street racing** - Racing exclusion applies
- **Damage to custom stereo equipment not permanently installed** - Electronic equipment exclusion
