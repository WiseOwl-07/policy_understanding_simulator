## Coverage Summary

This premium policy provides comprehensive All-Risk (Open Perils) coverage for your home and personal property, protecting against all risks of physical loss except those specifically excluded. This is the broadest form of homeowners coverage available.

---

## Section I - Property Coverages

### Coverage A - Dwelling
- **Limit:** $400,000
- **Coverage:** We cover the dwelling on the residence premises and structures attached to the dwelling on an **All-Risk (Open Perils)** basis.
- **All risks covered EXCEPT those specifically excluded** (see exclusions below)

**Exclusions:**
- Flood (from rising water or surface water - **but see Water Backup Coverage below**)
- Earthquake or earth movement (can be added by endorsement)
- Ordinance or law (covered up to 25% of Coverage A under this premium policy)
- Neglect or intentional loss
- War or nuclear hazard
- Power failure occurring off premises

### Coverage B - Other Structures
- **Limit:** $40,000 (10% of Coverage A)
- **Coverage:** We cover other structures on the residence premises separated from the dwelling (e.g., detached garage, shed, fence).
- **All-Risk coverage - same as Coverage A**

### Coverage C - Personal Property
- **Limit:** $200,000 (50% of Coverage A)
- **Coverage:** We cover personal property owned or used by an insured while anywhere in the world on an **All-Risk (Open Perils)** basis.
- **All risks covered** except specifically excluded perils

**Enhanced Special Limits on Personal Property:**
- $500 on money, coins, bank notes
- $5,000 on securities, stamps, valuable papers
- $3,000 on watercraft, trailers, and equipment
- $5,000 on jewelry, watches, furs (can schedule for higher limits)
- $5,000 on firearms
- $5,000 on silverware, goldware
- $10,000 on computer equipment and media

**Exclusions:**
- Animals, birds, or fish
- Motor vehicles or parts (except lawn mowers and equipment)
- Wear and tear, deterioration
- Insects, vermin, rodents (unless building collapse results)

### Coverage D - Loss of Use
- **Limit:** $80,000 (20% of Coverage A)
- **Coverage:** We cover Additional Living Expense if a loss makes your home uninhabitable.
- **Applies when loss is caused by any covered peril**

## Section II - Liability Coverages

### Coverage E - Personal Liability
- **Limit:** $300,000 per occurrence
- **Coverage:** We will pay damages for bodily injury or property damage for which an insured becomes legally liable due to an occurrence.

**What is NOT covered:**
- Intentional injury or property damage
- Business or professional activities
- Motor vehicle liability (covered by auto insurance)
- Property owned by or rented to the insured
- War or nuclear hazard

### Coverage F - Medical Payments to Others
- **Limit:** $5,000 per person
- **Coverage:** We will pay reasonable medical expenses incurred within 3 years of an accident for injury to persons on your property or caused by your activities.

**Exclusions:**
- Injury to an insured person
- Injury to persons regularly on premises for business purposes
- Injury from business or professional activities

## Section I - Enhanced Coverage Features

### Water Backup and Sump Pump Overflow Coverage
- **Limit:** $10,000 per occurrence
- **Coverage:** We cover direct physical loss to property caused by:
  - Water or waterborne material which backs up through sewers or drains
  - Water or waterborne material which enters and overflows from a sump pump, sump pump well, or other system designed to remove subsurface water
- **Deductible:** $500 (separate from main policy deductible)

**This enhanced coverage protects against common water damage scenarios not typically covered by standard policies.**

### Ordinance or Law Coverage
- **Limit:** Up to 25% of Coverage A ($100,000)
- **Coverage:** We cover the increased costs to comply with building codes when repairing or rebuilding after a covered loss:
  - Loss to undamaged portion of building
  - Cost to demolish and clear site
  - Increased cost to rebuild to current code

### Identity Theft Coverage
- **Limit:** $25,000
- **Coverage:** We reimburse expenses incurred to restore your identity, including:
  - Legal fees
  - Lost wages (up to $10,000)
  - Loan reapplication fees
  - Costs for certified mailings

### Equipment Breakdown Coverage
- **Limit:** $50,000
- **Coverage:** We cover direct physical damage to covered property caused by mechanical or electrical breakdown of:
  - HVAC systems
  - Water heaters
  - Built-in appliances
  - Electrical panels

## Section I - Perils Insured Against

### All-Risk (Open Perils) Coverage
This policy provides the **broadest coverage available**. We cover **ALL risks of direct physical loss** to your dwelling and personal property **EXCEPT** those specifically excluded below.

### Specific Exclusions:
1. **Flood from External Sources** - Damage from rising water, surface water, or groundwater (separate flood insurance required)
2. **Earthquake or Earth Movement** - Including landslide, mudflow, sinkhole (can be added by endorsement)
3. **Wear and Tear, Gradual Deterioration**
4. **Mold, Fungus, Wet Rot** - Except when resulting from a covered peril (limited coverage applies)
5. **Neglect** - Failure to protect property before or after a loss
6. **War or Nuclear Hazard**
7. **Intentional Loss**
8. **Power Failure** - Unless caused by damage to equipment on premises

**Important: Unlike standard policies that only cover named perils, this All-Risk policy covers everything else, including:**
- Accidental damage from any cause
- Mysterious disappearance (subject to proof of loss)
- Damage from unusual or unforeseen events

## Section I - Conditions

### Deductible
- **Amount:** $500 per occurrence (lower than standard)
- **Glass-only claims:** Deductible waived for glass breakage if no other damage
- We will pay only the amount of loss that exceeds the deductible.

### Duties After Loss
You must:
1. Give prompt notice to us or our agent
2. Protect the property from further damage (we'll reimburse reasonable expenses)
3. Prepare an inventory of damaged personal property
4. As often as we reasonably require, show the damaged property and provide records
5. Submit a signed, sworn proof of loss within 60 days after request

### Loss Settlement
- **Dwelling (Coverage A/B):** **Guaranteed Replacement Cost** - We will pay the full cost to repair or replace even if it exceeds the policy limit (within reason and subject to availability of materials)
- **Personal Property (Coverage C):** **Replacement Cost Coverage** (no depreciation) - We pay the cost to replace with new property of like kind and quality

## Common Coverage Scenarios

### ✅ Covered Scenarios:
- **House fire destroys multiple rooms** - Coverage A applies (after $500 deductible)
- **Lightning strike damages electrical system** - Coverage A applies
- **Burglar steals electronics and jewelry** - Coverage C applies (high special limits)
- **Windstorm tears off entire roof** - Coverage A applies
- **Sewer backs up and floods basement** - Water Backup Coverage applies (after $500 water backup deductible)
- **Hailstorm breaks windows** - Coverage A applies (no deductible for glass-only)
- **Temporary housing needed after fire** - Coverage D covers up to $80,000
- **HVAC system fails and causes damage** - Equipment Breakdown Coverage applies
- **Accidental damage to hardwood floors** - Covered under All-Risk (after deductible)
- **Child accidentally breaks TV** - Covered under All-Risk Personal Property (after deductible)

### ❌ Not Covered Scenarios:
- **Flood from nearby river overflows** - External flood exclusion; requires separate flood insurance
- **Earthquake cracks foundation** - Earth movement exclusion (endorsement available)
- **Roof deteriorates from age** - Wear and tear exclusion
- **Termite damage** - Vermin/insect exclusion
- **Intentional damage by homeowner** - Intentional loss exclusion

### Emergency Service Coverage
- Immediate coverage for emergency services (board-up, tarp, water extraction) up to $5,000 before deductible

## Important Policy Conditions

### Vacancy
If your home is vacant for more than 60 consecutive days, coverage is limited. Contact us for vacancy permits to maintain full coverage.

### Duties in Case of Loss
Failure to fulfill your duties after a loss may result in denial of your claim. You must:
- Notify us within a reasonable time
- Protect property from further damage (we'll reimburse you)
- Cooperate with investigation
- Submit proof of loss when requested
