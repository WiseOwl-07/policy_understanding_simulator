# Homeowners Insurance Policy - Policy Number: HOME-001-BOB

**Policyholder:** Bob Williams  
**Policy Period:** January 1, 2026 - January 1, 2027  
**Property Address:** 123 Maple Street, Springfield, IL  
**Policy Type:** HO-3 Special Form (Named Perils - Personal Property)

---

## Coverage Summary

This policy provides coverage for your home and personal property on a Named Perils basis, protecting against specific risks listed in this policy. Please review carefully to understand what is and is not covered.

---

## Section I - Property Coverages

### Coverage A - Dwelling
- **Limit:** $200,000
- **Coverage:** We cover the dwelling on the residence premises and structures attached to the dwelling.
- **Perils Covered:** Fire, lightning, windstorm, hail, explosion, riot, aircraft, vehicles, smoke, vandalism, theft, falling objects, weight of ice/snow/sleet, freezing, sudden accidental damage from artificially generated electrical current

**Exclusions:**
- Flood, surface water, or water below the ground surface
- Earthquake or earth movement
- Ordinance or law (requiring upgrades to code)
- Neglect or intentional loss
- War or nuclear hazard

### Coverage B - Other Structures
- **Limit:** $20,000 (10% of Coverage A)
- **Coverage:** We cover other structures on the residence premises separated from the dwelling (e.g., detached garage, shed, fence).
- **Same perils as Coverage A apply**

### Coverage C - Personal Property
- **Limit:** $100,000 (50% of Coverage A)
- **Coverage:** We cover personal property owned or used by an insured while anywhere in the world.
- **Named Perils Only:**
  1. Fire or lightning
  2. Windstorm or hail
  3. Explosion
  4. Riot or civil commotion
  5. Aircraft
  6. Vehicles
  7. Smoke
  8. Vandalism or malicious mischief
  9. Theft
  10. Falling objects
  11. Weight of ice, snow, or sleet
  12. Freezing

**Special Limits on Personal Property:**
- $200 on money, coins, bank notes
- $1,500 on securities, stamps, valuable papers
- $1,500 on watercraft, trailers, and equipment
- $2,500 on jewelry, watches, furs
- $2,500 on firearms
- $2,500 on silverware, goldware

**Exclusions:**
- Animals, birds, or fish
- Motor vehicles or parts (except lawn mowers and equipment)
- Property of roomers or boarders not related to the insured
- Articles separately described and specifically insured

### Coverage D - Loss of Use
- **Limit:** $20,000 (10% of Coverage A)
- **Coverage:** We cover Additional Living Expense if a loss makes your home uninhabitable.
- **Applies only when loss is caused by a covered peril**

---

## Section II - Liability Coverages

### Coverage E - Personal Liability
- **Limit:** $100,000 per occurrence
- **Coverage:** We will pay damages for bodily injury or property damage for which an insured becomes legally liable due to an occurrence.

**What is NOT covered:**
- Intentional injury or property damage
- Business or professional activities
- Motor vehicle liability (covered by auto insurance)
- Property owned by or rented to the insured
- War or nuclear hazard

### Coverage F - Medical Payments to Others
- **Limit:** $1,000 per person
- **Coverage:** We will pay reasonable medical expenses incurred within 3 years of an accident for injury to persons on your property or caused by your activities.

**Exclusions:**
- Injury to an insured person
- Injury to persons regularly on premises for business purposes
- Injury from business or professional activities

---

## Section I - Perils Insured Against

### Covered Perils (Dwelling - Coverage A/B):
1. **Fire** - Including damage from firefighting efforts
2. **Lightning**
3. **Windstorm or Hail** - Excluding damage to interior unless wind first creates an opening
4. **Explosion**
5. **Riot or Civil Commotion**
6. **Aircraft** - Including self-propelled missiles and spacecraft
7. **Vehicles**
8. **Smoke** - Sudden and accidental smoke from faulty operation of heating/cooking unit
9. **Vandalism or Malicious Mischief**
10. **Theft** - Including attempted theft
11. **Falling Objects** - Interior damage only if object first damages roof or exterior wall
12. **Weight of Ice, Snow, or Sleet**
13. **Freezing** - Sudden cracking/bursting of plumbing, heating, A/C, or sprinkler system

### Excluded Perils (NOT Covered):
- **Flood or water damage** from rising water, surface water, or groundwater
- **Earthquake or earth movement** including landslide, mudflow, or sinkhole
- **Sewer backup or sump pump overflow** (can be added by endorsement)
- **Water damage from continuous or repeated seepage or leakage**
- **Mold, fungus, or wet rot** (except as direct result of covered peril)
- **Wear and tear, deterioration, or gradual damage**
- **Neglect** - failure to protect property before or after a loss
- **Power failure** occurring off premises
- **War or nuclear hazard**

## Section I - Conditions

### Deductible
- **Amount:** $1,000 per occurrence
- We will pay only the amount of loss that exceeds the deductible.

### Duties After Loss
You must:
1. Give prompt notice to us or our agent
2. Protect the property from further damage
3. Prepare an inventory of damaged personal property
4. As often as we reasonably require, show the damaged property and provide records
5. Submit a signed, sworn proof of loss within 60 days after request

### Loss Settlement
- **Dwelling (Coverage A/B):** Replacement Cost (no depreciation) if you repair or replace within 2 years
- **Personal Property (Coverage C):** Actual Cash Value (depreciation applies) unless Replacement Cost Coverage is purchased

## Common Coverage Scenarios

### ✅ Covered Scenarios:
- **House fire destroys kitchen** - Coverage A applies (after $1,000 deductible)
- **Lightning strike damages electrical system** - Coverage A applies
- **Burglar steals TV and jewelry** - Coverage C applies (jewelry limited to $2,500)
- **Windstorm tears off roof shingles** - Coverage A applies
- **Vandals spray paint exterior walls** - Coverage A applies
- **Hailstorm breaks windows** - Coverage A applies
- **Temporary housing needed after fire** - Coverage D (Loss of Use) applies

### ❌ Not Covered Scenarios:
- **Flood damages basement** - Flood exclusion; requires separate flood insurance
- **Earthquake cracks foundation** - Earth movement exclusion
- **Sewer backs up into home** - Sewer backup exclusion (can add endorsement)
- **Slow water leak causes mold over time** - Gradual damage and mold exclusions
- **Roof deteriorates from age** - Wear and tear exclusion
- **Home left vacant for 3 months, pipes freeze** - Neglect/vacancy exclusion may apply

## Important Policy Conditions

### Vacancy
If your home is vacant for more than 60 consecutive days, certain coverages are suspended:
- Vandalism or malicious mischief
- Glass breakage
- Water damage
- Theft

### Duties in Case of Loss
Failure to fulfill your duties after a loss may result in denial of your claim. You must:
- Notify us promptly
- Protect property from further damage
- Cooperate with investigation
- Submit proof of loss
