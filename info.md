# Policy Understanding Simulator - Business Overview

## What is the Policy Understanding Simulator?

The **Policy Understanding Simulator** is an AI-powered application that translates complex insurance policy documents into clear, accessible answers for everyday coverage questions. Think of it as an intelligent assistant that reads insurance policies and explains coverage in plain English‚Äîinstantly.

### How It Works:
The system uses a **4-agent AI workflow** powered by advanced technologies:

1. **Scenario Interpreter** - Understands what the customer is asking about (asset, event, location)
2. **Policy Selector** - Determines which policy applies (Auto, Property, or Both)
3. **Retrieval Agent** - Searches actual policy documents using semantic AI search (RAG technology)
4. **Explanation Agent** - Generates clear, friendly explanations grounded in real policy language

The result: Customers get accurate answers like **"COVERED"**, **"NOT COVERED"**, or **"IT DEPENDS"** with professional explanations and specific policy citations‚Äîall in seconds.

## Common Customer Questions by Policy Type

Based on insurance industry research, here are the most frequently asked questions:

### **Auto/Car Insurance Questions:**

#### Coverage-Related
- "Am I covered if someone else drives my car?"
- "Does my insurance cover rental cars?"
- "What's covered under collision vs comprehensive?"
- "Am I covered if I hit an animal (deer, etc.)?"
- "Does my policy cover rideshare driving (Uber/Lyft)?"

#### Premium & Cost
- "Why did my premium go up?"
- "What factors affect my car insurance rate?"
- "Do I get a discount for multiple cars?"
- "How does my deductible affect my premium?"

#### Claims & Scenarios
- "What should I do after an accident?"
- "Will filing a claim raise my rates?"
- "Am I covered if my car is stolen?"
- "What happens if I'm hit by an uninsured driver?"

### **Property/Homeowners Insurance Questions:**

#### Coverage Scope
- "What's the difference between actual cash value and replacement cost?"
- "Is flood damage covered?" (Almost always NO - separate policy needed)
- "Does my policy cover water damage/leaks?"
- "Are personal belongings covered if stolen?"
- "Is earthquake damage covered?" (Usually NO - separate policy)

#### Specific Scenarios
- "Am I covered if a tree falls on my house?"
- "Does my policy cover mold?"
- "Are home-based business items covered?"
- "What if my tenant damages the property?"
- "Is dog bite liability covered?"

#### Policy Details
- "How much dwelling coverage do I need?"
- "What's liability coverage and how much should I have?"
- "Do I need additional coverage for expensive items (jewelry, art)?"
- "What's the difference between HO-3 and HO-5 policies?"

### **Cross-Policy Questions:**
- "What's an umbrella policy and do I need one?"
- "Can I get a discount for bundling auto and home?"
- "What's the difference between a deductible and a premium?"
